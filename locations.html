Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our locations and facilities.

Are all courses available at every location?

Most of our core courses are available at all locations. However, some specialized courses like Advanced AI or specific robotics programs may only be available at select branches. Please check the course details or contact your nearest branch for specific course availability.

Can I transfer between locations mid-course?

Yes, we offer flexibility for students to transfer between locations if needed. Please provide at least one week's notice to both the original and new location to ensure a smooth transition. All your progress and records will be transferred.

Do you provide parking facilities?

All our locations have either dedicated parking or arrangements with nearby parking facilities. Tashkent Center and Chilanzar branches have their own parking lots, while other locations have partnerships with nearby parking services.

Are the facilities accessible for people with disabilities?

Yes, all our locations are equipped with ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms. We are committed to providing an inclusive learning environment for all students. If you have specific accessibility requirements, please contact us in advance so we can ensure the best possible experience.

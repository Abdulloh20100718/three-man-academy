About CoddyCamp IT Academy

CoddyCamp is a premier IT academy dedicated to teaching coding and technology skills to children and teenagers aged 7-17. Our curriculum is inspired by Harvard's CS50 course, adapted for different age groups to ensure an engaging and effective learning experience.

We offer both online and offline courses in programming, design, and artificial intelligence, taught by experienced instructors who are passionate about technology education.

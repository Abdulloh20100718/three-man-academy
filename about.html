About CoddyCamp

Empowering the next generation of tech innovators through quality education

Our Story

CoddyCamp was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate tech professionals and educators who saw the need for specialized IT education for young people. What started as a small coding club has grown into a full-fledged academy with multiple locations across Uzbekistan.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to make technology education accessible, engaging, and effective for children and teenagers. We believe that early exposure to coding and digital skills can open doors to countless opportunities in the future.

Over the years, we have helped thousands of students discover their passion for technology and develop skills that will serve them throughout their academic and professional careers.

Our Education Methodology

Inspired by Harvard's CS50 and modern pedagogical approaches, our methodology focuses on practical learning, problem-solving, and project-based education.

Learn by Doing

Our courses emphasize hands-on projects and practical exercises that reinforce theoretical concepts. Students spend 70% of their time coding and creating, not just listening.

Project-Based Learning

Students work on real-world projects that challenge them to apply their skills and knowledge. Each course culminates in a final project that students can proudly showcase.

Collaborative Environment

We foster teamwork and peer learning through group projects and coding challenges. Students learn to communicate their ideas and work effectively with others.

Personalized Approach

We adapt our teaching methods to each student's learning style, pace, and interests. Small class sizes ensure that every student receives individual attention.

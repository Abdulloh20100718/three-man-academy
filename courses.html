Frequently Asked Questions

What equipment do students need for online courses?

Students will need a computer (Windows, Mac, or Linux) with a stable internet connection. Specific software requirements will be provided before the course starts, and most software used is free or has educational licenses.

How are students grouped in classes?

Students are grouped by age (7-9, 10-14, 15-17) and experience level. We ensure that each student is placed in a class where they can learn effectively and comfortably.

Can I switch from online to offline classes or vice versa?

Yes, for hybrid courses, students can switch between online and offline attendance based on their needs. Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance if you plan to change your attendance mode.

What happens if my child misses a class?

All classes are recorded and made available to enrolled students. Additionally, our instructors offer make-up sessions and extra help for students who miss classes.

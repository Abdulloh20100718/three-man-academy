Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How can I enroll my child in a course?

You can enroll your child by filling out the application form on our website, visiting one of our branches in person, or calling our enrollment hotline. After submitting your application, our team will contact you to confirm enrollment and provide further details.

What age groups do you cater to?

Our courses are designed for students aged 7-17, with specific age ranges for each course. We have courses for ages 7-9, 10-14, and 15-17, each tailored to the appropriate skill level and learning pace.

Do you offer both online and offline classes?

Yes, we offer both online and offline classes for most of our courses. Some specialized courses may only be available in one format. You can check the format availability on each course's description page.
